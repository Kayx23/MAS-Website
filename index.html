McMaster Actuarial Society

Who Are We

Established in 2012, the McMaster Actuarial Society (MAS) is dedicated to bringing together the vast actuarial and mathematics community on campus. Our society's functions include supplying students with actuarial exam resources, providing information about the actuarial accreditation process, hosting employer recruitment sessions and social events, mentoring students on actuarial career development, representing McMaster Actuarial Science at Conferences/Case Competitions, and more.
